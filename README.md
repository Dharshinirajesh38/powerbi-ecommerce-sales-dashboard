# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce sales performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. The dashboard helps identify revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

## Dataset

The dashboard uses two datasets:

- Orders.csv
- Details.csv

These datasets contain information related to orders, sales amount, profit, quantity, product categories, sub-categories, customer details, and payment methods.

## Objectives

The primary objectives of this dashboard are to:

- Monitor overall business performance.
- Analyze sales and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing states and customers.
- Evaluate product category and sub-category performance.
- Understand customer payment preferences.
- Support business decision-making through data insights.

## Business Questions Answered

1. Which states contribute the most revenue?
2. Which product categories perform best?
3. What are the monthly profit trends?
4. Who are the top customers?
5. Which payment modes are most preferred?
6. Which sub-categories generate the highest sales?
7. What business opportunities can be identified from the data?

## Key Insights

- Maharashtra recorded the highest sales (₹102K).
- Clothing is the highest-selling category, contributing 62.62% of total sales.
- Printers are the best-performing sub-category.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method, accounting for 43.74% of transactions.
- Profit was negative during May, June, September, and December.
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to overall sales revenue.

## Executive Summary

- This dashboard highlights the overall performance of the e-commerce business across multiple dimensions, including sales, profit, customer behavior, and product performance.

- The analysis reveals that Maharashtra is the strongest revenue-generating state, while Clothing dominates category sales. Printers emerge as the highest-performing sub-category, and Cash on Delivery remains the preferred payment method among customers.

- Although overall business performance is positive, profit declines during certain months indicate opportunities for operational improvements and cost optimization.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing states such as Maharashtra.
- Investigate the causes of profit declines during May, June, September, and December.
- Expand inventory and promotional campaigns for top-selling products and categories.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through discounts, cashback offers, and loyalty programs.
- Focus on retaining high-value customers who contribute significantly to overall sales.

## Storytelling Insights

The dashboard tells the story of an e-commerce business with strong sales performance driven by specific regions, product categories, and customer segments. While revenue generation remains healthy, fluctuations in monthly profit highlight areas that require strategic attention. These insights can help stakeholders improve profitability, optimize inventory planning, and strengthen customer engagement strategies.
