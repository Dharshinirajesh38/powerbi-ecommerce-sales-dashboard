# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into e-commerce sales performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Quantity Sold
- Customer Performance
- Product Categories
- Payment Methods
- Monthly Profit Trends

## Business Questions Answered

1. Which states contribute the most revenue?
2. Which product categories perform best?
3. What are the monthly profit trends?
4. Who are the top customers?
5. Which payment modes are most preferred?
6. Which sub-categories generate the highest profit?

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Revenue: ₹438K
- Total Profit: ₹37K
- Total Quantity Sold: 5,615
- Average Order Value: ₹121K

## Key Insights

- Maharashtra leads in total sales.
- Clothing is the highest-selling category.
- Printers generate the highest profit among sub-categories.
- COD is the most preferred payment method.
- Profit declines during May–August.
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to sales.
